FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about joining SBS.

SBS is open to motivated students from any faculty and academic year, as long as you are willing to learn and commit to the work.

You can mention more than one interest in your application, but we’ll focus on matching you with the committee where you can grow the most.

Experience helps but is not required. We care more about your mindset, commitment, and willingness to learn step by step.

After the interview phase, we’ll send an email with the final decision and next steps for accepted applicants.
