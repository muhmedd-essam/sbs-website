About SBS

SBS – Step By Step is a student activity focused on turning potential into progress. We create a space where students can learn, experiment, and grow together.

Skill Development

Build technical and soft skills through curated workshops, learning paths, and mentoring from experienced members.

Real Experience

Work on real initiatives, campus events, and cross‑functional projects that feel like a startup environment.

Community & Leadership

Join a supportive community, lead teams, and learn how to collaborate, communicate, and drive ideas forward.

What We Do

A mix of technical, soft skills, and experiential learning to help you grow in every dimension.

Workshops

Hands‑on workshops that take you from basics to building real things in code, design, and more.

Technical Sessions

Deep‑dive sessions on engineering topics, tools, and best practices delivered by peers and guests.

Soft Skills Training

Training on communication, teamwork, presentation, and personal branding to boost your presence.

Events & Competitions

Hackathons, challenges, and on‑campus events that push you to think, build, and compete.
